Tortilla Soup

Description

With January on the horizon we are well into soup season. Warm up with this spicy, savory and flavorful dish that is simple to make and easy to dress up with fun condiments to your liking. Add pickled red onions, shredded cheese, sour cream or avocado to really make this soup pop. Your friends and family will love it and you'll love making it!

Ingredients

  • White or yellow onion
  • Green bell pepper
  • Red/yellow/orange bell pepper
  • Hot peppers of your choosing (I like to use a few jalapenos, serrano and anaheims work well too)
  • Canned black beans, three bean blend, diced tomatoes
  • Beef or chicken stock/bouillion
  • Salt, pepper, chili powder, cayenne pepper, cumin, smoked paprika

Steps

  1. Finely chop onion and all of your peppers and add to a pan with your preferred cooking oil; season with spices.
  2. While you continue to stir and season your veggies, add all of the canned goods to a large stock pot with either your stock or boullion and bring to a boil.
  3. Once your veggies are a nice consistency add them to your stock and simmer one to two hours
