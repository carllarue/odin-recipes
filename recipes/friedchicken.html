Air Fried Chicken

Description

Who doesn't love fried chicken? Make a healthier, less messy version in your air fryer and enjoy one of my favorite guilty pleasures with a little more innocense.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Bisquick, panko, chili powder, paprika
  • Cooking spray
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Pat your chicken dry with a paper towel.
  2. Dredge chicken thoroughly in a mixture of bisquick and spices.
  3. Dip the dredged chicken into a bowl of whipped eggs.
  4. Complelety cover the chicken with panko.
  5. Preheat your air fryer to 370 degrees
  6. Set your chicken in the fryer basket and cook for 15 minutes or until cooked through and golden brown (larger pieces of chicken may require more time than that, I like to trim mine into smaller pieces for a quicker cook)
