Enchiladasagna

Description

Enchiladas are amazing. Casseroles are amazing. Enchilada casserole would be an appropriate name, but I always think of this dish as lasagna-esque, so to me it has always been and will always be that: "Enchiladasagna".

Ingredients

  • Onions, bell peppers, spicy peppers
  • Corn tortillas
  • Shredded cheese
  • Black beans
  • Enchilada sauce of your choice
  • Optional: Olives, protein like pulled chicken or ground pork

Steps

  1. Cook down your onions and peppers with a healthy amount of seasoning; I like cumin and paprika.
  2. Add black beans to the veggies once everything is lightly browned and tender. Reseason to taste.
  3. In a 2-3 inch deep casserole dish, put down a layer of corn tortillas.
  4. Dollop 1/3 of your bean/onion/pepper mixture over top of the tortillas.
  5. Add a generous amount of shredded cheese and enchilada sauce, completely covering this layer.
  6. Repeat those steps to add a total of three layers, covering the third layer with tortillas and topping with more cheese and enchilada sauce.
  7. Bake for 30-45 mintues at 400 degrees or until the top is golden brown and crispy.
